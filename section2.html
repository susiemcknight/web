A Beginner's Guide to Soccer

Top Tournaments

There are many elite and popular soccer leagues and tournaments that occur across the world. Some of these tournaments are the CONCACAF Gold Cup, UEFA European Championship, and the FIFA World Cup, which occurs every four years for both men and women. Some of the best professional leagues in the world are English Premier League, Serie A, La Liga, Bundesliga, and the Major League Soccer, which takes places here in the US.
