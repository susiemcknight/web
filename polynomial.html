My Favorite Polynomial

I chose the polynomial y=7x^3-11x^2+5x-13 because although it may look like it, I didn't want to chose random coefficients. I decided to make all of the numbers in the equiation prime and relatively small.In determining whether each term should be positive of negative, I simply alternated between adding and subtracting them. This polynomial has four terms for no particular reason as I just made it in the middle of the number of terms required. Overall, this equation is not too complex, but the coefficients would make the equation much harder to solve due to their prime nature.
